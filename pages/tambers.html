tamber's

Tamber's is a local restaurant and carryout treasured by the Baltimore community. After falling in love with their food, I pulled up the website and realized how dated it was. I decided to give the brand a makeover with logo re-design & website re-design. I then brought the designs to life with html, css & javascript. Finally, I envisioned the future of their brand promotion by designing and coding an html email that introduces the restaurant and offers a discount.

problem

This restaurant's website is dated and its navigation is not intuitive. The restaurant also does not have many approachable ways to connect to its users beyond calling directly or walking in.

the makeover

a new brand image

This website and logo design went through several iterations. I browsed restaurant websites across region and cuisine to identify the most useful and common features (in site menus, hours, restaurant history) made available to users. The design for the logo was inspired by a street style thali--a staple Indian cuisine--to speak to the restaurant's position as a diner than can also serve Indian food on the go.

the website

This single-page website concisely features critical information such as the restaurant's hours and location, history, and menus.

*I did not include the full menu to the website, as it features hundreds of items.
