Spirit Fashion Show

Spirit Fashion show is a student-run fashion show that attracts participants and audience members across the Pittsburgh area. As students come together to put on a show full of fashion, entertainment, and visual statements publicity becomes vital to recruit members and introduce the show to members of the campus and local community. As the marketing coordinator, I launched a solo, wide-scale marketing initiative in which I: designed a logo, built the organization's first website*, created content for & managed the website and social media platforms, designed posters, and more.

Logo design

I wanted to create a logo that spoke to the organizations black multicultural origins & highlight the fashion show's theme of the year.

Website

At the time. The organization did not have a website. This site contained information about the organization, a list of its members, recurring blog post, and a contact form.
